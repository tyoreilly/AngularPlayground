Executive Summary

DBRS, Inc. (DBRS) has finalized its provisional ratings on California Republic Auto Receivables Trust 2016-1 (CRART 2016-1) as listed on page one. The CRART 2016-1 transaction represents California Republic Bank's (CRB or the Company) twelfth securitization of auto loans.

Management has demonstrated the ability to manage growth in the auto business. Growt h in the portfolio has been supported by continued investment in personnel and systems to enhance the servicing platform to meet growth objectives.
